CASUAL & LAID-BACK


Mood: Relaxed, comfortable
Occasion: Day out, brunch, shopping


Description: Casual & Laid-back is all about effortless comfort while still looking stylish. It's perfect for a relaxed day out, brunch with friends, or a casual shopping trip. The focus is on simplicity, ease, and understated fashion that feels good to wear all day.
Outfit: Think of an oversized t-shirt or a loose-fitting sweater paired with mom jeans or denim shorts. Complete the look with classic white sneakers for comfort and a crossbody bag for practicality. This aesthetic often leans towards more relaxed silhouettes and soft, breathable fabrics like cotton or linen.
Colors: Neutral tones (beige, white, grey), pastels (light pink, soft blue), or earthy hues (olive green, tan) work well to keep the look easygoing and laid-back.
Accessories: Minimalist jewelry like a delicate necklace or small hoop earrings add just the right touch without being too flashy. Sunglasses, a baseball cap, or a simple watch can elevate the outfit while maintaining the casual vibe.
This aesthetic is perfect for days when you want to be comfortable but still look put together with minimal effort.


CHIC & POLISHED


Mood: Sophisticated, stylish
Occasion: Office, business meetings, formal lunch

Outfit: Tailored blazer, high-waisted trousers, silk blouse, block heels.
Colors: Monochrome or muted tones (beige, navy, black). Accessories: Statement earrings, structured bag, sleek hairstyle.
Description: Chic & Polished is a refined and sophisticated aesthetic that exudes elegance while maintaining a sense of style. It’s perfect for occasions that require a more professional or formal look, such as business meetings, office wear, or a stylish lunch with colleagues. The key is to blend classic, tailored pieces with modern details for a clean, elevated appearance. Outfit: This look is all about structure and sharp lines. Think tailored blazers, high-waisted trousers, pencil skirts, or a well-fitted dress. You can pair these with a silk or satin blouse, creating a smooth and elegant silhouette. Finish with block heels or pointed-toe flats for a touch of sophistication while staying comfortable.
Colors: Stick to monochrome or muted tones like black, navy, beige, white, or grey for a sleek look. You can add a pop of color with accessories but keep the overall palette understated and professional.
Accessories: Structured bags (like a tote or a clutch), statement earrings, and a sleek watch add polish to the outfit. Think of a minimalist yet impactful approach—delicate jewelry or a bold statement piece like a single cuff bracelet can work wonders. A neat hairstyle, like a low bun or sleek blowout, adds to the polished effect.
The Chic & Polished aesthetic is all about striking a balance between sophistication and simplicity, allowing you to appear effortlessly put-together and stylish.


ROMANTIC & FEMININE


Mood: Dreamy, gentle
Occasion: Date night, weddings, special dinners

Outfit: Flowing floral dress, kitten heels, delicate cardigan or shawl.
Colors: Soft pinks, lavender, blush, and creams.
Accessories: Pearl jewelry, lace detailing, dainty rings.
Description:Romantic & Feminine is a dreamy, delicate aesthetic that highlights soft, graceful elements. This style is perfect for occasions that call for elegance and charm, such as date nights, weddings, or special dinners. It focuses on flowing silhouettes, floral patterns, and gentle fabrics that evoke a sense of whimsy and tenderness. Outfit: Think flowing dresses or skirts made of chiffon, lace, or silk. Ruffles, lace detailing, and floral prints often feature prominently in this style. Dresses with sweetheart necklines, puff sleeves, or wrap designs are common choices. Pair the look with kitten heels or ballet flats to keep it elegant and comfortable. Lightweight cardigans or shawls can be added to the ensemble for an extra touch of softness.
Colors: Soft and pastel tones like blush pink, lavender, cream, baby blue, or warm beige are popular in this aesthetic. These light, delicate shades enhance the romantic feel of the outfit. Accessories: Dainty accessories like pearl earrings, delicate necklaces, and fine rings add to the feminine vibe. Floral hairpieces, subtle headbands, or ribbon-tied ponytails can also complement the look. A small clutch or handbag with lace or satin details completes the ensemble. For makeup, soft, dewy looks with rosy cheeks and pink lips are ideal.
The Romantic & Feminine aesthetic is all about embracing beauty and softness, creating a look that is elegant yet understated with a timeless, graceful appeal.


EDGY & BOLD


Mood: Rebellious, adventurous
Occasion: Night out, concerts, casual events


Description:The Edgy & Bold aesthetic is daring and unapologetic, perfect for those who want to make a statement. It often incorporates rebellious fashion elements and is ideal for concerts, night outs, or casual settings where you want to showcase your fearless style. Outfit: This look often includes leather jackets, ripped jeans, graphic tees, or distressed clothing. You might pair these with combat boots or platform shoes for an extra tough edge.
Colors:Dark and moody tones dominate this style—think black, deep red, or metallic shades like silver or gunmetal.
Accessories:Chunky chains, studded belts, and bold makeup (like smoky eyes or dark lipstick) define this look. A statement bag or layered rings can complete the ensemble, adding a sense of raw power.
The Edgy & Bold aesthetic is about pushing boundaries and owning your individuality with fierce confidence.

BOHEMIAN & FREE-SPIRITED


Mood: Carefree, artistic
Occasion: Music festivals, beach days, road trips



Description: The Bohemian & Free-Spirited aesthetic is all about embracing creativity, earthiness, and a carefree attitude. This style is ideal for music festivals, beach trips, or any event where you want to channel an effortless, artistic vibe.
Outfit: Flowy maxi skirts or dresses, crochet tops, wide-leg pants, and crop tops are typical. Fringe details, floral prints, and embroidered patterns are also common. Footwear often includes strappy sandals or espadrilles.
Colors: Earth tones like rust, mustard, olive green, and brown, mixed with bright, nature-inspired hues or patterns, give this aesthetic its grounded feel.
Accessories: Layered necklaces, stacked bracelets, floppy hats, and woven bags are key to completing the look. Incorporate natural textures like leather, wood, and feathers. Messy waves or loose braids and natural makeup add to the effortless vibe.

SPORTY & ACTIVE


Mood: Energetic, on-the-go
Occasion: Gym, yoga class, outdoor activities

Description: The Sporty & Active aesthetic is designed for those who are always on the go, blending functionality with fashion. It’s perfect for gym sessions, yoga classes, or any casual day when comfort and movement are a priority.
Outfit: Think leggings, joggers, sports bras, and moisture-wicking tops. A zip-up hoodie or windbreaker adds layers for warmth, and running shoes or trainers complete the look.
Colors: Bold neon shades, cool blues, black, or color-blocked designs are common in this aesthetic. Athleisure brands often incorporate striking logos or stripes for a sporty finish.
Accessories: A fitness tracker, baseball cap, or duffel bag are perfect accessories to keep the outfit practical. Sleek ponytails or braids work well for a polished, active look.
The Sporty & Active aesthetic is for those who love combining high performance with a trendy, on-the-move vibe.

GLAMOROUS & LUXE


Mood: Confident, extravagant
Occasion: Red carpet, upscale events, galas

Description: The Glamorous & Luxe aesthetic is all about extravagance and opulence. It’s perfect for high-end events, red carpets, or fancy dinners where you want to shine and turn heads.
Outfit: Think of sequined gowns, satin dresses, or velvet ensembles. You can opt for form-fitting silhouettes or flowy, dramatic gowns with plunging necklines or high slits. Stilettos or embellished heels complete the luxe look.
Colors: Rich jewel tones like emerald green, ruby red, and royal blue, as well as metallic shades (gold, silver, bronze), make up this aesthetic's palette.
Accessories: Statement pieces are a must—think chandelier earrings, oversized cocktail rings, and a sparkling clutch. Hair and makeup should also be bold, with sleek waves, glamorous updos, and dramatic makeup like red lips and glowing skin.
The Glamorous & Luxe aesthetic is for those who love to feel like royalty and aren’t afraid to bring the drama in their wardrobe choices.

MINIMALIST & SLEEK


Mood: Clean, focused
Occasion: Work meetings, art galleries, formal gatherings

Description: The Minimalist & Sleek aesthetic is all about clean lines and understated elegance. It’s perfect for art galleries, formal gatherings, or professional environments where simplicity speaks louder than embellishment.
Outfit: Opt for well-tailored, simple pieces like a structured blazer, high-waisted trousers, or a minimalist dress. Fitted coats, like trench coats, or blouses with subtle detailing also work well. Simple pointed flats or minimalist heels complete the look.
Colors: Stick to neutral shades like black, white, grey, and beige. Minimalism thrives on monochromatic palettes and solid colors.
Accessories: Minimalist jewelry such as thin gold chains, simple stud earrings, or a sleek wristwatch. A structured handbag or tote adds functionality without overwhelming the simplicity of the look.
The Minimalist & Sleek aesthetic is for those who value quality and precision in their wardrobe, focusing on timeless pieces that create a polished, refined appearance without excess.
